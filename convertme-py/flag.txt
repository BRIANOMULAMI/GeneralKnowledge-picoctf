    Divide 69 by 2 → 69 ÷ 2 = 34, remainder 1

    Divide 17 by 2 → 17 ÷ 2 = 8, remainder 1

    Divide 4 by 2 → 4 ÷ 2 = 2, remainder 0
    Divide 2 by 2 → 2 ÷ 2 = 1, remainder 0
